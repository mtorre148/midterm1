X-File Movie and Show Reviews

Reviews about the hit show X-Files

X-Files: Fight the Future

What can be said of the first movie. It fit the arcs needed to engage fans as much as possible. It centered on the larger arc of the series and not one of the smaller arcs. It also developed the relationships in not only the FBI, but between Mulder and Scully as well. Great movie even for the casual X-Fan.

X-Files: I want to believe

X-Files Series Review

Hands down, what I consider to be the greatest Television show of all time. 11 Seasons of great, keep you on the edge of your seat TV that holds up even today. What makes the series great are the arcs. There are Series Arcs (The larger Conspiracy), season arcs (Abductions, Plane Crashes), and one-off shows. Not to be forgotten, these one-off shows didn't speak to the larger arcs, but they ran the gamut of all your senses. There were scary, macabre episodes, even holiday episodes, funny episodes, serious ones etc. The show is perfect, its not mystery that many of the show's alums went on to create even more of what are considered the greatest shows on television.
